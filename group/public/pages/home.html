Your top questions

How do I borrow?

You can browse through the listed items on our platform and send a request to borrow. Once the owner approves, we’ll facilitate the exchange by confirming pickup or delivery details. Payment or deposit (if required) will be processed securely through our system.

Is it free?

Creating an account and browsing is free. However, small service fees may apply when you borrow or lend an item—these cover platform maintenance, verification, and support services. All costs will be shown clearly before confirming any transaction.

How to lend?

Simply create a listing by uploading clear photos, adding a short description, and setting your lending terms (such as duration and price). Once approved, your item will appear for borrowers to request. You’ll receive notifications when someone is interested.

What about damage?

We encourage both parties to inspect items before and after each transaction. If damage occurs, our support team will review the situation and mediate a fair resolution. Depending on the agreement, repair or replacement costs may be covered by the borrower or through our protection policy.
